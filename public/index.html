Features

Everything you need,
nothing you don't

AU Bank CSV Import

Supports CBA, ANZ, Westpac, NAB, ING, Bankwest and more. Drag, drop, and you're done.

Smart Categorisation

Keyword rules auto-sort your transactions. Tap any row to recategorise or add notes and tax tags.

Monthly Overview

Navigate month by month. See totals by category, a live pie chart, and your safe-to-spend amount.

Import History

Every CSV you import is logged. Made a mistake? Delete an import and all its transactions disappear.

Per-User Accounts

Each person gets their own private database. Your data stays yours, separate from everyone else's.

Duplicate Detection

Import the same CSV twice by accident? Immagini spots exact duplicates and skips them automatically.
